Emotional regulation is not about suppressing or ceasing to feel emotions. It is about managing how we react to them and what we do with the information they provide. 감정 조절은 감정을 억누르거나 느끼지 않으려는 것이 아닙니다. 감정이 주는 정보를 어떻게 받아들이고, 그에 어떻게 반응하느냐를 다스리는 것입니다.

How does it work

When a feeling arises, if you fight it it will persist. However, if you observe it, it will pass by calmly.
어떤 감정이 올라올 때 그것과 싸우면 오히려 더 오래 머뭅니다. 그러나 그 감정을 바라보면, 그 감정은 조용히 지나가게 됩니다.

Step 1: Observe

What am I really feeling?

To regulate our emotions we must understand them first

Think about something that is currently troubling you
or has troubled you in the past

Take a few seconds to think and then continue

How does it make you feel?

Once we name our emotions,
we begin to truly feel them

Step 2: Validate

Don’t judge the emotion, embrace it

Emotions are signals that point you in the right direction

Step 3: Regulate

Now that you named the emotion,
let's calm things down

Step 4: Integrate

Choose the right strategy for you,
